# WorkNow - Project Vision & Core Strategy

## Tagline
Casual Jobs. Real Pay. Safe and Simple.

---

## Mission
WorkNow replaces handwritten job signs, WhatsApp broadcasts, and informal hiring methods with a trusted digital marketplace for casual work.

---

## Problem
Millions of Nigerian workers and employers lack a trusted platform to connect safely, quickly, and fairly.
*   **Employers** struggle to find reliable workers.
*   **Workers** struggle to find verified jobs and often face unpaid work, fraud, and safety risks.

---

## Solution
WorkNow is a safety-first casual job marketplace that allows employers to post jobs and workers to find, apply for, and complete work with guaranteed payment through escrow.

---

## Core Pillars
1.  **Safety:** Active monitoring, SOS button, location check-ins, and safety notifications.
2.  **Verification:** Mocked Identity (NIN) verification checks to ensure users are who they say they are.
3.  **Escrow Payments:** Locked milestones via a mock OPay integration to guarantee worker payouts.
4.  **AI-Powered Trust:** Automated review summaries, safety threat detection, and smart moderation logs.
5.  **Accessibility:** Mobile-first, low friction, easy interfaces tailored for Nigerian mobile users.

---

## Primary Users

### Workers
*   Students looking for quick gigs.
*   Casual labourers.
*   Artisans (electricians, plumbers, painters).
*   Event workers (waitstaff, cleaners, decorators).

### Employers
*   Shop owners.
*   Event planners.
*   Contractors.
*   Households.
*   Small businesses.

---

## What WorkNow Is Not
*   **LinkedIn:** No corporate networking focus, resume-building, or professional content feeds.
*   **Upwork / Fiverr:** Not for long-term remote digital freelance contracts or international bidding.
*   **Remote Work Marketplace:** Tailored for local, physical, or immediate casual work in Nigeria.
*   **Corporate Recruitment Platform:** No white-collar hiring tracks or long screening pipelines.

---
